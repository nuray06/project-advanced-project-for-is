About project

JobScope KZ is a data-driven dashboard for exploring the IT job market in Kazakhstan.

Problem

Students and junior specialists often do not know which IT profession matches their skills and what skills are most demanded in Kazakhstan.

Objective

Analyze IT vacancies, show salary and skill trends, and recommend matching jobs using an AI-style career assistant.

Stack

Flask, Pandas, Chart.js, Plotly, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, CSV dataset, and resume text extraction.

Innovation

The system connects skills, vacancies, salaries, cities, and recommendations in one interactive dashboard.
