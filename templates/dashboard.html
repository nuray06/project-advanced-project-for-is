AI Career Assistant

Напиши свои навыки или загрузи резюме (.txt/.pdf), чтобы получить профессию и подходящие вакансии.

О проекте

Problem

Students and junior specialists often do not know which IT profession matches their skills and what skills are demanded in Kazakhstan.

Objective

Analyze IT vacancies, show salary/skill trends, and recommend matching jobs using a career assistant.

Stack

Flask, Pandas, Chart.js, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, CSV dataset, PyPDF2 for optional resume PDF extraction.

Innovation

AI-style assistant: skills/resume → profession → matching vacancies → missing skills to improve.
