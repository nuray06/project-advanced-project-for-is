About Project

Problem

Students and junior specialists often do not know which IT profession matches their skills and what skills are demanded in Kazakhstan.

Objective

Analyze IT vacancies, show salary/skill trends, and recommend matching jobs using an AI-style career assistant.

Stack

Flask, Pandas, Chart.js, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, CSV dataset, PyPDF2, and lightweight TF-IDF similarity logic.

Innovation

AI-style assistant: resume → profession prediction → skill gap → salary prediction → matching vacancies.
